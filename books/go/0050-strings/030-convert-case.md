* [`strings.ToLower`](https://golang.org/pkg/strings/#ToLower)

      fmt.Println(strings.ToLower("FOOBAR")) // foobar

* [`strings.ToUpper`](https://golang.org/pkg/strings/#ToUpper)

      fmt.Println(strings.ToUpper("foobar")) // FOOBAR
